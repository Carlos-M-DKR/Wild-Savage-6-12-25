WILD WOLF

Schnauzers are smart, loyal dogs with a distinctive beard and bushy eyebrows.
They come in three sizes: Miniature, Standard, and Giant.
  • Schnauzers were originally bred in Germany to catch rats and guard farms.
  • They don't shed much, which makes them a good choice for people with allergies.
